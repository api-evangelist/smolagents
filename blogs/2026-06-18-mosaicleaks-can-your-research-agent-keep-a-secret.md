MosaicLeaks is a benchmark measuring how deep research agents leak private enterprise data across intent leakage, answer leakage, and full-information leakage, exploiting the "mosaic effect" where benign queries collectively reveal sensitive information. Training agents with Privacy-Aware Deep Research (PA-DR) reduces answer and full-information leakage from 34% to under 10% while maintaining a 58.7% task success rate.
