PP-OCRv6 is the latest generation of PaddleOCR's universal OCR model family for text detection and recognition across documents, screenshots, multilingual images, digital displays, industrial labels, and scene text. The model family scales from 1.5M to 34.5M parameters with support for 50 languages.
